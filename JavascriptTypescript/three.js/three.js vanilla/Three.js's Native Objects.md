## THREE

Definiremos como THREE:
~~~js
import * as THREE from 'trhee' 
~~~

* **THREE.BoxGeometry()** → Creates a simple Box 3d Geometry
* **THREE.SphereGeometry()** → Creates a basic Sphere 3d Geometry
